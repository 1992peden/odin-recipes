Lemon Tofu

Description

Do you like protein? Do you like sugar?Do you like CITRUS???

You should have answered yes to all of those questions. Make this recipe.

Ingredients

  • 15 oz. (1 block) extra firm tofu
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt & Pepper to taste
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce or gluten-free Tamari
  • 1/3 cup agave syrup
  • Juice of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • 1/2 cup vegetable broth
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1 tbsp water
  • Steamed rice and/or veggies to serve over

Instructions

  1. Press the tofu for at least 15 minutes beforehand.
  2. Preheat oven to 425 degrees Farenheit.
  3. Cut the block of tofu into cubes, and toss in olive oil and a few pinches of salt and pepper.
  4. Place tofu in a single layer on a sheet pan. Bake for 30-40 minutes, flipping over halfway through baking.
  5. Heat a wok or sauce pan on medium high, then add the soy sauce, agave, lemon juice, and veggie broth. Whisk to combine.
  6. Bring to a simmer, then reduce heat to medium low. Whisk corn starch and water together in a small bowl. Add corn starch slurry to the sauce, whisk to combine.
  7. Simmer on low for 1-2 minutes, continuing to whisk, until sauce is thickened. Taste and adjust seasoning.
    • If the sauce needs salt, add a splash of soy or tamari.
    • If the sauce needs sweetness, add more agave syrup.
    • If the sauce needs more lemon, squeeze more lemon juice into the pan.
  8. Once tofu is done and sauce is ready, add the tofu to the pan or wok and toss to combine.
  9. Serve immediately over rice and/or vegetables.
  10. Enjoy!
